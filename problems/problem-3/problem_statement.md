### Problem Statement

Write a code in languages (C/C++/Python/Java or any other language you wish) such that for the given input you get the given output.

#### Test Case 1
**input**

4 2 5 1 3 6

4 5 2 6 3 1

**output**

1 2 4 5 3 6

#### Test Case 2

**input**

19 13 1 69

19 13 69 1

**output**

1 13 19 69
